Protein Smoothie

Description

Kick your hunger to the curb with this bountiful smoothie rich in antioxidants and protein. Perfect for a busy morning or post-workout replenishment to give your metabolism a boost whilst meeting all your nutrient needs. Measurements can be scaled up to be used as a meal replacement, or scaled down to drink as a supplement. Whichever you choose, the smoothie is guaranteed to tantalize your taste buds and provide a boost of energy.

Mix & match your favorite fruits to match the season, and try different combinations of protein sources to make this smoothie your very own.

Ingredients

  • Fruit of choice

    If you're looking for a creamy texture, bananas are best. For a juicier experience, you can't go wrong with an array of berries. Get creative and try different flavors & combinations!

  • Protein powder of choice

    If you have a more sensitive stomach, I recommend powder without whey as these can sit heavily. Whichever you choose, make sure there is an adequate amount of protein, preferably 25g or more. I like to change up the powder depending on whether I need a muscle recovery boost after a hard work-out or a nutrition boost to replace a sit down meal.

  • Protein-rich milk, preferably flax or nut based

    Karma makes a great flaxseed milk with a whopping 8g of protein in each cup. Silk also has protein-rich nut or soy milks, some with more protein than others. While the 20g soy protein milk is packed with protein, it has a thicker consistency and is best paired with a lighter milk such as cashew or flaxseed. It is best to avoid almond milks as these are not very sustainably sourced.

  • Garnish of choice

    Boost your flavor pallet with a drizzle of honey, agave syrup, or chocolate swirl. This is optional but definitely provides a flavor boost!

  • Water & ice

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients and chop and peel as necessary
  2. In a large enough blender, place 2 cups of chopped fruit or 1 whole fruit, depending on choice
  3. Add 1-2 scoops of protein powder
  4. Pour 3-4 cups of milk
  5. Add a few ice cubes and 1/2 cup of water
  6. Drizzle in your garnishes (optional)
  7. Blend ingredients together, using smoothie setting or frozen fruit setting
  8. Voila! Pour into your favorite glass and enjoy the tangy, protein-packed goodness that your whole body will enjoy
