Epic Veggie Salad

Description

If you are anything like me, you understand the struggle of finding a hearty salad full of protein and nutrition that meets the daily needs of a vegetarian diet.

Well, look no further, because this salad is both filling and delicious that provides a protein boost to fuel those muscles. Allow yourself the creative freedom to mix & match different flavors and ingredients to match any occasion or nutrition need.

Whether it's a salad to give you some afternoon energy, or a meal to recover from a work-out, benefit from the nourishment this salad provides while keeping to a clean, vegetarian diet.

Ingredients

  • Green base: lettuce, kale, spinach, etc
  • Assortment of vegetables of choice: tomatoes, carrots, broccoli, peppers, cucumbers, peas, beans, radish, beets, cabbages, corn kernels, etc. Have fun with it! Bonus if garden grown
  • Nuts: cashews, almonds, macadamia, etc. Give your salad of boost of good fats
  • Fruits: avocado (yes! a fruit), apples, grapes, mandarin, etc. Add a burst of tangy goodness
  • Meatless protein source: cubed tofu, sliced seitan, crumbles, "chicken" breast, etc. Combine a few to give a variety of protein goodness
  • Scoop of hummus (optional)
  • Dressing of choice, or use a simple oil and lemon spritz to bring out the flavors of ingredients
  • Salad mix seasoning, salt, pepper, chili flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients together, wash, mix, and chop as needed
  2. In a large salad bowl, add your green base
  3. Top with chopped fresh veggies of choice. Make it a colorful assortment to add an aesthetic touch
  4. Sprinkle in your nuts of choice
  5. Add your fruits of choice
  6. Add protein source(s) of choice
  7. Add a dollop of hummus (optional)
  8. Sprinkle any seasonings you want. I love to use a salad mix seasoning that includes sesame & poppy seeds with onion and garlic salt. If you like a spice kick, sprinkle in some chili flakes or a dash of cayenne pepper.
  9. Drizzle in your dressing of choice, but considering the flavor packed ingredients, dressing is not necessary!
  10. Mix together thoroughly and voila! You have a jam-packed salad that will fill you up and provide a boost of nutrition. Have fun with it and be creative! There is no right or wrong way to make this delicious salad.
